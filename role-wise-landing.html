Built around thoughtful progress

The screenshots show a product that feels calm, structured, and sequential. Instead of pushing users to spray applications, Role Wise guides them through setup, matching, search, generation, prep, and tracking with a clear sense of what to do next.

Guide-led from the start

The home screen centers a Guide that helps users decide what to do next, whether that means tailoring a resume, running a search, or reviewing tracker tasks. That makes the product feel more like a job-search system than a collection of tools.

Fit before volume

Role assessment creates the decision framework first, and search results are then scored against it. That reinforces the brand promise behind Role Wise: helping users choose better, not just apply faster.

One place for the full loop

The app includes setup, warm connections, search, application generation, prep and follow-up, and a tracker. That continuity is one of the product’s strongest differentiators and should stay central in the copy.

A workflow that matches the product

The copy below mirrors the actual screen flow: profile and settings, guided setup, warm connections, role assessment, job search and evaluator, application generator, prep and follow-up guide, and job tracker.

01

Set up your profile once

Start with your resume, then add supporting documents like strengths assessments, performance reviews, writing samples, and other career materials. Role Wise uses that foundation to understand both your background and your voice.

02

Map your warm connections

Upload your LinkedIn connections CSV and Role Wise highlights where you already know someone. That adds practical network context to search and prioritization without asking users to log into LinkedIn through the app.

03

Build your role assessment

Before you start applying, Role Wise defines what good fit means for you. That creates a stable matching framework that later screens and materials can use consistently.

04

Run a search the right way

Use Quick Scan, Balanced, or Deep Search depending on how broad you want to go. The job search and evaluator screens make it clear that search is configurable, deliberate, and grounded in user criteria.

05

Generate only for strong matches

Select the roles worth pursuing, then generate the needs analysis, resume, cover letter, and related materials in one step. The app’s application generator makes that transition from search to action feel tight and organized.

06

Prepare and follow through

Role Wise carries the same context into interview prep, follow-up drafting, and the tracker. That means users do not need to rebuild context every time they move to the next phase of a search.

Why “Role Wise” fits

The screenshots suggest a product that is measured, practical, and supportive. “Role Wise” works because it sounds like better judgment, not hustle, and because the interface itself reinforces that through guided steps, saved context, and steady progress.

What the name captures

  • A wiser way to decide which roles deserve attention.
  • A calmer alternative to generic application automation.
  • A system that helps users move from signal to action to follow-up.

What the UI reinforces

  • Guide-led prompts instead of a blank productivity dashboard.
  • Role assessment and search depth controls that favor judgment over volume.
  • Tracker statuses and follow-up tools that support the whole search, not just the first application.
