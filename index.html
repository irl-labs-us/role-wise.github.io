What RoleWise helps you do

Three steps: get clear on what you want, apply well, and stay on top of it.

Step 1

Know what you’re aiming for

Start with a guided assessment that pulls in your background, skills, education, and strengths, plus insights from past career coaches or reflections.

Step 2

Apply with intent

Turn that assessment into tailored resumes, cover letters, and outreach templates for each role, without rewriting everything from scratch.

Step 3

Stay organized across the search

Track companies, applications, interviews, and follow-ups in one place, so you always know what’s next and what needs attention.

Why I built RoleWise

The story behind the product.

Angela Orthmeyer
Founder, RoleWise

When I was out of a job, I was juggling dozens of applications, follow-ups, and half-finished resume drafts in scattered docs and spreadsheets.

I was using AI, notes from career coaches, books, and my own research to figure out what I should be pursuing, but nothing brought it all together into a clear plan.

RoleWise is my attempt at solving that: it helps you get clear on what you want, turns it into a practical assessment, and uses it to guide your search and applications from day one.

Make your job search more intentional.

Start with a quick RoleWise assessment and get a clearer path to roles that fit, then let the Guide keep you on track with tailored applications and timely follow-ups.
